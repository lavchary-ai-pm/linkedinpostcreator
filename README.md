# LinkedIn Post Creator

A web app that generates engaging LinkedIn posts tailored to your target audience using AI.

## Features

- **AI-Powered Content Generation**: Uses advanced AI to create authentic LinkedIn posts
- **Audience Targeting**: Customizable for different professional audiences
- **Easy Copy-to-Clipboard**: One-click copy functionality
- **Professional UI**: Beautiful, responsive design

## Supported Audiences

- Tech Professionals
- Entrepreneurs & Founders
- Marketing Professionals
- Job Seekers & Career Changers
- Business Leaders & Executives
- Educators & Trainers
- Sales Professionals
- Product Managers
- UX/UI Designers
- Data Analysts & Scientists

## How to Use

1. Enter your topic (e.g., "AI in recruiting", "Remote work culture")
2. Select your target audience
3. Click "Generate Post"
4. The app will generate a professional LinkedIn post
5. Click "Copy Post" to copy to clipboard

- **Frontend**: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
